Our Story & Legacy

From Fruits To Ice Cream, A Journey Of Originality

Founder

Late Shri Raghunandan Kamath

Founder, Naturals Ice Cream

Born in a small village near Mangalore, Shri Raghunandan Kamath learned the art of selecting quality fruits while helping his father sell fruits.

His belief was simple — if ice cream is made from fruits, then fruits should remain the hero. This philosophy led to the creation of Naturals Ice Cream in 1984.

Our Legacy

40+ Years Of Original Taste

Naturals began with a single store in Mumbai and a dream to create authentic fruit-based ice creams.

Today the brand is loved across India and continues to follow the same philosophy of simplicity, freshness and originality.

Every scoop celebrates real fruits, real ingredients and honest craftsmanship.

Our Vision

To preserve the natural goodness of fruits and deliver unforgettable experiences through every scoop of ice cream.
